What You Will Learn

Golf may seem simple, but with a deeper understanding of the sport one can learn much more and enjoy to play and watch the sport. The information presented on this page will assist one in learning: what is golf, and how do you play?

Golf

In simplest terms, golf is a sport where you hit a ball with a stick. The objective of golf is to hit the ball into the hole. With this basic information, one needs to know that the "sticks" are called clubs. Throughout the hole, one must use different clubs, such as the driver, irons, and a putter to eventually have the ball fall into the hole.
