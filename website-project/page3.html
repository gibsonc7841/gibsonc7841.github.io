Every subject has professionals, and that is no different with golf. Here is some information on professional play and a little on the professionals themselves.

Golf's Professionals

Golf has some well known professional aspects, such as the PGA Tour or the majors - the four most prestigious events in professional golf.

First, let's talk about the PGA Tour. The tour was founded in 1929, and organizes most of the main events that are primarily between male professionals. Jay Monahan is currently the fourth commissioner of the tour.

Next up, the majors. These four major tournaments are the Masters, the U.S. Open, the Open Championship and the PGA Championship. Jack Nicklaus currently holds the most major championship wins at a whopping 18.

This year's (2017) Masters title went to Sergio Garcia, and the Open title going to Jordan Spieth.
